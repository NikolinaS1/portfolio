About me

My name is Nikolina Stjepanović

I am 21 years old and I am studying computer engineering at the Faculty of Electrical Engineering, Computer Science and Information Tehnology in Osijek. In my free time I like to paint and draw. I'm not a professional, drawing is my hobby. The techniques I use the most are pencil, oil and ink. I showed an interest in art as a child, but I started drawing at the age of 19. In the gallery you will be able to see some drawings created in the last two years.
